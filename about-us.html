About Us.

Maud and Ivy, what does it mean and who are we?

Maud is a nickname I have had since childhood and Ivy is my daughter’s name and also a beautiful plant I like to work with. The name just seemed like a beautiful choice to me.

When you look deeper into the meanings of these names, they become more apt. Maud is an old name signifying a “powerful battler”. Historically Ivy wreaths were presented to couples as a sign of “fidelity and faithfulness”. My son Nate also holds a special role with Maud and Ivy Flowers as he is in charge of quality control. Just like me, he has a great eye for detail and he oversees all my work. Together we are powerful, dependable and devoted to providing great service and work.

My father was a creative man, being one of the Gold Coast’s leading sign writers and sign makers and an artist entertaining people with his humorous sketches. Until now, nobody has followed in his creative footsteps. I decided to completely change direction in life due to injury and believe it has been a blessing and I have found what I am meant to do.

Creating gives me great pleasure. Handing over beautiful work for any occasion is very satisfying. Contemplating how I can make each and every arrangement more personal is essential to my process. I pride myself on taking

We aim to provide personalised service resulting in stylish and extraordinary work for all occasions.
