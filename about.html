Our Foundations.

Maud and Ivy,
what does it mean and who are we?

Maud is a nickname I have had since childhood and Ivy is my daughter’s name and also a beautiful plant I like to work with. The name just seemed like a beautiful choice to me.

When you look deeper into the meanings of these names, they become more apt. Maud is an old name signifying a “powerful battler”. Historically Ivy wreaths were presented to couples as a sign of “fidelity and faithfulness”.

My son Nate also holds a special role with Maud and Ivy Flowers as he is in charge of quality control. Just like me, he has a great eye for detail and he oversees all my work. Together we are powerful, dependable and devoted to providing great service and work.

My real name is Monique and I was born on the Gold Coast in 1971 and have enjoyed living here my entire life.
